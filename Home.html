-Elizabeth Rona

Just like exercising your body leads to increased strength and flexibility, working on your mind and brain using my cutting edge approach will lead to increased strength, clarity, resilience, and lightness of being.


I have been practicing for 31years and specialize in treating post-traumatic stress disorder, attachment issues in relationships, grief, anxiety, depression, and addictive patterns.


My approach to therapy is rooted in neurobiology, psycho-neuroimmunology and energy medicine and integrates Somatic Experiencing®, Interpersonal Neurobiology (IPNB) and EMDR into “talking therapy.” I focus on how both suffering and a sense of well being influence the Mind-Body-Spirit connection.


I will help you learn to regulate and manage feelings that overwhelm you and negatively affect your thoughts, moods, and attitudes, and that, in turn, lead to unwanted behavior. Healing can occur in a therapeutic relationship, through the awareness that is born out of mindfully observing yourself while being witnessed by another.


I work with individuals, couples, families, teens and children ages 7 and up, and have worked extensively with military service members returning from Iraq and Afghanistan, focusing on war experiences and their influences on their relationships and families.


I am fluent in English, Spanish and German and understand the significant effects one’s culture has on personal experiences and relationships.


Personally, I am guided by 15 years of meditation; marshal arts, and a life-long practice of yoga and dance.  


You can make an appointment in my office in Los Angeles or via telephone, skype or i-chat.
